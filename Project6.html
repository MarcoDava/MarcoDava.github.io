Math Mania

Timeline : Janurary 2023 - March 2023
This is a simple GUI math quiz game that would time users and track scores. It is able to track usernames and store their scores into a file as a leaderboard system. My group split work amongst ourselves and I was in charge of putting all our functions togehter.


How I did it:

We used the JavaFX Framework and designed the GUI. We had one person focus on the design of it while the rest of us worked on backend. On top of putting all the functions together, I designed the timer of the game. I had everyone email their respective codes to me and I would figure out a way to split them into their classes. Using these classes, I was able to connect all the functions together seamlessly


Mistakes Made:

The problems I faced was the mismatching logic in all our functions. the team did not come to a collective design before the assignment, thus, we had code that did not work well with each other. We had many group meetings to come up with how to solve with the problem. I had to change a lot of things however, I came to a successful finish.
